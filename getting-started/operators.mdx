# Getting Started for Operators

Ready to work on real projects, level up your career, and unlock long-term opportunities? Here's how to join the Startery ecosystem.

## The Launchpad Process

**Launchpad** is our 4-week builder sprint in collaboration with [Antler](https://antler.co) that vets new operators.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply">
    Apply via the "Become an Operator" or "Launchpad" link at [startery.io](https://startery.io)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Weekly Sprints">
    Complete weekly deliverables and demonstrate your skills through real-world projects
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Mentorship">
    Get guidance from experienced operators and Antler partners
  </Step>
  <Step title="IRL Hackathon">
    Attend the live hackathon finale to showcase your work
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join the Network">
    Top performers get fast-tracked into the Startery Talent Network or Antler's residency
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to Expect

### During Launchpad
- **Weekly deliverables** to complete on time
- **Mentorship** from experienced operators
- **Real projects** to build your portfolio
- **Community** of fellow builders
- **IRL hackathon** finale event

### After Qualifying
- **Paid project opportunities** through Bountyboard
- **Career progression** through talent levels
- **Full-time hiring eligibility** (Associate level and above)
- **Guild collaboration** on complex projects

  </Accordion>
