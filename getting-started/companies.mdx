# Getting Started for Companies

Ready to accelerate your execution with elite operators? Here's how to get started with Startery.

## Quick Start Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit startery.io">
    Go to [startery.io](https://startery.io) and enter your project in the input field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Processing">
    Our Scoping Agent will begin processing your request and transforming it into structured deliverables.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Proposal">
    You'll see a sample roadmap, Guild preview, and quote for your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Started">
    Click "Start working on this" to book a call or fill out onboarding to begin your project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
