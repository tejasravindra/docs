# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from companies considering or using Startery.

## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What kind of work can I get done on Startery?">
    Startery supports a wide range of work types including:
    - **Strategy**: Market research, business planning, competitive analysis, go-to-market strategy
    - **Design**: UI/UX design, branding, design systems, user research, prototyping  
    - **Product**: Product management, roadmapping, feature specification, user testing
    - **Engineering**: Frontend, backend, mobile development, DevOps, technical architecture
    - **Operations**: Process optimization, automation, data analysis, project management
    - **Research**: Market research, user research, competitive intelligence, data analysis
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get started?">
    1. Go to [startery.io](https://startery.io)
    2. Enter your project in the input field
    3. Our Scoping Agent will process your request
    4. Review the sample roadmap, Guild preview, and quote
    5. Click "Start working on this" to book a call or fill out onboarding
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Starter and Growth plans?">
    **Starter Plan** ($1,000/month + $1-3K per project):
    - Perfect for early-stage teams
    - Async-only communication
    - One deliverable at a time
    - No long-term commitment
    
    **Growth Plan** ($8K-12K/month based on commitment):
    - Unlimited project requests
    - 1-2 active projects at a time
    - Dedicated engagement manager
    - Weekly strategy syncs
    - Private Slack channel
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Project Execution

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How much time/money does Startery save?">
    **Time Savings**: 4-6 weeks saved compared to traditional hiring (sourcing, interviewing, onboarding)
    
    **Cost Savings**: 30-50% reduction in total cost for scoped projects compared to full-time employee overhead
    
    **Quality Assurance**: All operators are pre-vetted through our Launchpad program
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I'm not happy with the work?">
    We offer multiple safeguards:
    - **Operator replacements** if there's not a good fit
    - **Direct communication** with an engagement manager
    - **Iterative sprint cycles** with built-in feedback loops
    - **Quality assurance** at each milestone
    - **Revision cycles** to ensure deliverables meet your standards
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do you ensure quality?">
    - **Rigorous vetting**: All operators complete our 4-week Launchpad program
    - **Engagement managers**: Dedicated oversight for every project
    - **Structured delivery**: Sprint-based milestones with clear success criteria
    - **Performance tracking**: Continuous monitoring of operator performance
    - **Client feedback**: Regular check-ins and satisfaction surveys
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long do projects typically take?">
    Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity:
    - **Small projects**: 1-2 weeks
    - **Medium projects**: 2-4 weeks  
    - **Large projects**: 4-8 weeks
    - **Complex initiatives**: 8+ weeks with Guild teams
    
    Our Scoping Agent provides accurate timeline estimates during project planning.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pricing & Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I have to commit to a long-term contract?">
    **Starter Plan**: No long-term commitment. Pay monthly access fee + per-project costs.
    
    **Growth Plan**: Commitment options available:
    - 3 months: $12K/month
    - 6 months: $10K/month  
    - 12 months: $8K/month
    
    Longer commitments offer better pricing and priority access.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does billing work?">
    - **Monthly access fees**: Billed upfront at the beginning of each month
    - **Project costs**: Billed upon project completion and your approval
    - **Hiring fees**: Only if you choose to hire an operator full-time
    - **No hidden fees**: Transparent pricing with no surprise charges
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    - Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
    - ACH bank transfers
    - Wire transfers for international clients
    - Net 30 terms available for Growth plan clients
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?">
    - **Starter Plan**: Cancel anytime with no penalty
    - **Growth Plan**: Pro-rated refunds available in the first 30 days
    - **Project-level**: We work to ensure satisfaction before final billing
    - **Hiring fees**: Success-based, only charged upon successful placement
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Hiring & Team Building

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if I want to hire an operator full-time?">
    You absolutely can! Our hiring process is designed for operators you've worked with:
    
    - **Eligibility**: Associate level and above operators who've completed projects with you
    - **Fees**: 5% from company + 5% from operator at hire, plus 7.5% each after 1-year retention
    - **Process**: Express interest to your engagement manager, we handle logistics
    - **Advantage**: You've already validated fit through real project work
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I build a dedicated team through Startery?">
    Yes, through several approaches:
    - **Guild deployment**: Pre-formed teams for complex projects
    - **Sequential hiring**: Hire multiple operators you've worked with successfully
    - **Hybrid model**: Mix of project-based operators and full-time hires
    - **Custom team assembly**: We can help build teams for specific needs
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if an operator I want to hire isn't interested?">
    All hiring decisions require mutual agreement. Some operators prefer:
    - Continued project-based work for flexibility
    - Working with multiple companies
    - Pursuing other opportunities
    
    We'll help you find alternative operators with similar skills and proven fit.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical & Operational

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do the AI agents work?">
    Our AI agents enhance the entire process:
    
    - **Scoping Agent**: Transforms your requests into clear deliverables and timelines
    - **Staffing Agent**: Matches projects with optimal operators based on skills and availability  
    - **PM Agent**: Tracks progress, identifies blockers, and ensures quality
    - **Execution Agents** (coming soon): AI takes first pass on certain tasks
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a Guild and when do I need one?">
    **Guilds** are small, functional teams (2-4 operators) for complex projects requiring multiple skill sets:
    
    - Strategy + Design for go-to-market initiatives
    - Design + Engineering for product development
    - Product + Operations for system implementations
    
    **When to use Guilds:**
    - Multi-disciplinary projects
    - Tight timelines requiring parallel work
    - Complex initiatives needing coordinated expertise
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do you handle different time zones?">
    - **Async-first workflow**: Designed for distributed collaboration
    - **Global operator network**: Coverage across all major time zones
    - **Flexible communication**: Mix of real-time and asynchronous updates
    - **Time zone matching**: We can prioritize operators in your preferred zones
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What tools and platforms do you use?">
    - **Bountyboard**: Our main project management platform
    - **Slack**: For Growth plan clients with dedicated channels
    - **Standard tools**: Whatever your team already uses (GitHub, Figma, etc.)
    - **File sharing**: Secure cloud storage with access controls
    - **Communication**: Email, video calls, and in-platform messaging
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
