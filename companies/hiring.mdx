# Hiring Through Startery

Transform successful project collaborations into permanent team members. Startery's unique "try before you buy" approach ensures cultural and skill fit before making long-term commitments.

## How Hiring Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Work Together First">
    Collaborate with operators through Bountyboard projects to assess fit, working style, and quality.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Identify Great Fits">
    Operators at Associate level and above become eligible for full-time hiring after successful project completion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Express Interest">
    When you find an operator you'd like to hire, simply notify your engagement manager or through the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Facilitate Introduction">
    We handle the transition logistics, including compensation discussions and start date coordination.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Seamless Onboarding">
    Since you've already worked together, onboarding is streamlined and efficient.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Hiring Fees

Transparent, success-based pricing structure:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Initial Hire">
    **5% fee from company + 5% from operator**
    
    - Applied at time of hire
    - Based on first-year compensation
    - One-time fee for successful placement
    - Only for operators you've worked with
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Retention Milestone">
    **Additional 7.5% from both sides**
    
    - Applied after 1-year retention milestone
    - Ensures long-term success for all parties
    - Demonstrates sustained value and fit
    - Incentivizes quality placements
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
**No upfront fees.** You only pay when you successfully hire an operator you've worked with, ensuring complete alignment of interests.
</Note>

## Hiring Process FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does the hiring process take?">
    Since you've already worked together, the process typically takes 1-2 weeks for logistics and paperwork completion.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if the operator doesn't want to be hired?">
    All hiring decisions require mutual agreement. Operators may prefer to continue project-based work or have other commitments.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I hire operators who haven't worked on my projects?">
    No. Our hiring program is exclusively for operators you've collaborated with through Startery projects to ensure proven fit.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What about non-compete or contract obligations?">
    We handle all transition logistics and ensure proper contract termination and new agreement setup.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a minimum project duration before hiring?">
    No minimum duration, but we recommend completing at least one full project cycle to properly assess fit.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
