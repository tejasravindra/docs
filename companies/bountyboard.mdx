## How Bountyboard Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit Your Project">
    Describe what you need done. Our system accepts everything from high-level
    goals to detailed specifications.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Scoping">
    The **Scoping Agent** analyzes your input and creates: - Clear project scope
    and deliverables - Timeline and milestone structure - Resource requirements
    and skills needed - Success criteria and acceptance requirements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Smart Matching">
    The **Staffing Agent** matches your project with: - Individual operators
    with the right skills - Guilds (small teams) for complex projects - Optimal
    availability and capacity - Track record of similar successful projects
  </Step>
  <Step title="Managed Execution">
    Your dedicated **Engagement Manager** ensures: - Regular progress updates
    and check-ins - Quality assurance at each milestone - Clear communication
    and feedback loops - Timeline adherence and scope management
  </Step>
</Steps>
