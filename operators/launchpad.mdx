**Launchpad** is our 4-week builder sprint in collaboration with [Antler](https://antler.co) that vets new operators and fast-tracks them into the Startery Talent Network.

## What is Launchpad?

Launchpad is more than just an application process—it's a **transformative 4-week experience** where aspiring operators prove their skills through real-world projects while receiving mentorship from industry experts.

## What You'll Experience

### Real-World Projects

Work on actual challenges submitted by companies in our network:

- **Strategy projects**: Market analysis, competitive research, go-to-market planning
- **Design challenges**: UI/UX design, brand development, user research
- **Engineering tasks**: Technical architecture, prototype development, system design
- **Operations projects**: Process optimization, automation design, data analysis

### Expert Mentorship

Get guidance from experienced operators and Antler partners:

- **1:1 mentorship sessions** with industry experts
- **Group workshops** on specialized skills
- **Office hours** for project-specific guidance
- **Peer learning** with fellow Launchpad participants

### Network Access

Connect with the broader Startery and Antler ecosystem:

- **Alumni network** of successful operators
- **Company partnerships** for potential opportunities
- **Investor connections** through Antler's network
- **Global community** across multiple markets
