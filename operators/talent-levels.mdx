## Detailed Level Breakdown

### Apprentice

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Profile" icon="seedling">
    **High school/early undergrad level**

    New operators entering the ecosystem with foundational skills and strong learning potential.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Characteristics" icon="list">
    - Completing or recently completed secondary education
    - Strong foundational skills in chosen area
    - Eager to learn and grow
    - Requires guidance and mentorship
    - Not yet client-facing independently
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Typical Projects:**

- Research and analysis tasks
- Design asset creation under supervision
- Basic development tasks with mentorship
- Documentation and process work

**Advancement Criteria:**

- Complete 3\+ supervised projects successfully
- Demonstrate consistent quality improvement
- Show initiative and learning agility
- Receive positive mentor feedback

---

### Analyst

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Profile" icon="chart-simple">
    **Sophomore/junior level**

    Building foundational professional skills with increased independence and responsibility.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Characteristics" icon="list">
    - Undergraduate level education or equivalent experience
    - Developing specialized expertise
    - Can work independently on defined tasks
    - Beginning to interface with clients
    - Contributing to team success
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Typical Projects:**

- Independent research and analysis
- Feature design and development
- Client communication under guidance
- Process improvement initiatives

**Advancement Criteria:**

- Complete 5\+ projects with minimal supervision
- Demonstrate client communication skills
- Show technical/domain expertise growth
- Receive client satisfaction ratings \>4.0/5

---

### Associate ⭐

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Profile" icon="user-check">
    **Senior/early grad level**

    Professional skills with proven delivery capability. **First level eligible for full-time hiring.**
  </Card>
  <Card title="Characteristics" icon="list">
    - Senior undergraduate or graduate level
    - Client-ready communication skills
    - Independent project delivery
    - Quality assurance mindset
    - **Hiring eligible** by companies they've worked with
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Typical Projects:**

- End-to-end project ownership
- Direct client interface and management
- Complex technical or strategic deliverables
- Cross-functional collaboration

**Advancement Criteria:**

- Complete 8\+ projects as primary contributor
- Achieve \>4.5/5 client satisfaction consistently
- Demonstrate leadership potential
- Show business acumen development

**Hiring Benefits:**

- Eligible for full-time hiring by client companies
- Can negotiate direct employment opportunities
- Access to higher-value project assignments

---

### PM (Project Manager)

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Profile" icon="clipboard-list">
    **Early-career, client-ready**

    Project management experience with client-facing responsibilities and leadership capabilities.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Characteristics" icon="list">
    - Proven project management skills
    - Excellent client communication
    - Team coordination abilities
    - Strategic thinking development
    - **Full hiring eligibility**
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Typical Projects:**

- Multi-operator project coordination
- Client relationship management
- Strategic planning and execution
- Cross-functional team leadership

**Advancement Criteria:**

- Successfully manage 5\+ complex projects
- Lead teams of 2-4 operators
- Demonstrate business impact
- Receive leadership feedback \>4.5/5

---

### EM (Engineering Manager)

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Profile" icon="users-gear">
    **Senior lead with business acumen**

    Engineering management and senior leadership capabilities with strategic business understanding.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Characteristics" icon="list">
    - Senior leadership experience
    - Technical and business expertise
    - Team building and development skills
    - Strategic business impact
    - **Premium hiring eligibility**
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Typical Projects:**

- Large-scale project leadership
- Technical architecture and strategy
- Team building and development
- Business strategy consultation

**Advancement Criteria:**

- Lead 10\+ major initiatives successfully
- Develop and mentor junior operators
- Demonstrate significant business impact
- Achieve exceptional client outcomes

---

### Partner

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Profile" icon="handshake">
    **Stakeholder with equity/revenue share**

    Business partnership potential with proven strategic value and ownership mindset.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Characteristics" icon="list">
    - Equity/revenue sharing opportunities
    - Strategic business partnership
    - Long-term commitment to Startery
    - Exceptional track record
    - **Executive hiring potential**
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Typical Projects:**

- Strategic business initiatives
- New market development
- Partnership and BD work
- High-stakes client relationships

**Partnership Benefits:**

- Equity participation in Startery
- Revenue sharing from projects
- Strategic decision-making input
- Priority access to opportunities

---

### Elder

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Profile" icon="crown">
    **Advisory board or master operator**

    Industry expertise and mentorship capabilities with master-level skills and wisdom.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Characteristics" icon="list">
    - Industry expertise and recognition
    - Master-level technical or business skills
    - Mentorship and advisory capabilities
    - Strategic vision and guidance
    - **Advisory and consulting roles**
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Typical Projects:**

- Strategic advisory engagements
- Mentorship of junior operators
- Industry thought leadership
- High-level consulting projects

**Elder Benefits:**

- Advisory board participation
- Thought leadership opportunities
- Premium consulting rates
- Industry recognition and speaking

## Advancement Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Performance Review">
    Regular evaluation of project quality, client feedback, and skill development.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Peer Assessment">
    360-degree feedback from colleagues, mentors, and clients.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Skill Demonstration">
    Practical demonstration of next-level capabilities through project work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mentorship">
    Guidance from higher-level operators to prepare for advancement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Level Promotion">
    Official recognition and access to new opportunities and responsibilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>
