### Bounty Creation Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Project Input">
    Describe your project needs in natural language—from high-level goals to detailed specifications.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Analysis">
    Scoping Agent processes your input, asks clarifying questions, and structures requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Proposal Generation">
    Receive detailed proposal with scope, timeline, operator recommendations, and pricing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Approval">
    Review the structured proposal, request modifications, and approve to begin execution.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Operator Matching">
    Staffing Agent presents matched operators or Guilds with rationale and track records.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Project Kickoff">
    Automated introductions, shared workspace setup, and milestone tracking activation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
