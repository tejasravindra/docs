Startery operates as a dual-sided marketplace connecting companies with elite operators through a structured, AI-enhanced workflow.

## For Companies

<Steps>
  <Step title="Post a Bounty">
    Submit your project via our Bountyboard platform. Whether it's strategy,
    design, product, engineering, or operations work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Scoping">
    Our **Scoping Agent** transforms your request into clear, structured
    deliverables with defined scope and timeline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Smart Staffing">
    The **Staffing Agent** matches your project with the optimal Guild of vetted
    operators based on skills and availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Managed Delivery">
    An **Engagement Manager** ensures quality, pacing, and delivery through
    structured sprint cycles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Iterate">
    Receive deliverables, provide feedback, and iterate. Optionally hire
    operators full-time if there's a vibe match.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For Operators

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply to Launchpad">
    Join our 4-week builder sprint in collaboration with Antler. Complete weekly
    deliverables and receive mentorship.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Sprints">
    Demonstrate your skills through real-world projects, building your portfolio
    and reputation within the network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join the Network">
    Top performers gain access to paid projects and the Startery Talent Network
    with structured career progression.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Level Up">
    Progress through talent levels: Apprentice → Analyst → Associate → PM → EM →
    Partner → Elder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unlock Opportunities">
    At Associate level and above, become eligible for full-time hiring by
    companies you've worked with.
  </Step>
</Steps>
