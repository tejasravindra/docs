# Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Date]

At Startery, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform and services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number, professional title
- **Profile Data**: Skills, experience, education, portfolio items, work history
- **Communication Data**: Messages, comments, feedback, and other communications
- **Payment Information**: Billing address, payment method details (processed by third-party providers)

### Project Information
- **Project Details**: Requirements, specifications, deliverables, timelines
- **Work Product**: Files, documents, code, designs, and other project outputs
- **Performance Data**: Quality ratings, feedback, completion metrics
- **Communication Records**: Project-related discussions and correspondence

### Technical Information
- **Usage Data**: Platform activity, feature usage, session duration
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Cookies**: Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies
- **Log Data**: Access logs, error logs, performance metrics

## How We Use Your Information

### Service Provision
- **Platform Operation**: Account management, authentication, service delivery
- **Project Matching**: AI-powered matching between companies and operators
- **Quality Assurance**: Performance monitoring, feedback collection, improvement
- **Communication**: Platform messaging, notifications, updates

### Business Operations
- **Analytics**: Usage analytics, performance measurement, optimization
- **Security**: Fraud prevention, security monitoring, platform protection
- **Legal Compliance**: Legal obligations, dispute resolution, enforcement
- **Marketing**: Service improvements, feature announcements (with consent)

## Information Sharing

### With Other Users
- **Public Profiles**: Skills, experience, ratings (for operators)
- **Project Collaboration**: Shared workspace access for team members
- **Feedback**: Performance reviews and ratings (anonymized when appropriate)

### With Third Parties
- **Service Providers**: Payment processing, analytics, infrastructure
- **Legal Requirements**: Court orders, legal processes, regulatory compliance
- **Business Transfers**: Mergers, acquisitions, asset sales (with notice)

### Never Shared
- **Private Communications**: Direct messages, private feedback, personal notes
- **Financial Details**: Complete payment information, earnings data
- **Proprietary Work**: Client IP, confidential project information

## Data Security

### Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption**: End-to-end encryption for sensitive communications
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
- **Infrastructure**: SOC 2 compliant hosting, regular security audits
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring, incident response procedures

### Operational Safeguards
- **Employee Training**: Regular privacy and security training
- **Data Minimization**: Collect only necessary information
- **Retention Limits**: Automatic deletion of unnecessary data
- **Vendor Management**: Strict security requirements for third parties

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Access and Control
- **Account Settings**: Update profile, preferences, communication settings
- **Data Export**: Download your data in machine-readable format
- **Data Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Correction**: Update or correct inaccurate information

### Communication Preferences
- **Email Communications**: Opt-out of marketing emails
- **Platform Notifications**: Customize notification settings
- **Third-Party Marketing**: Opt-out of data sharing for marketing

### Regional Rights (GDPR, CCPA)
- **Access**: Right to know what data we have about you
- **Portability**: Right to receive your data in portable format
- **Deletion**: Right to have your data deleted ("right to be forgotten")
- **Objection**: Right to object to certain processing activities

## Cookies and Tracking

### Essential Cookies
- **Authentication**: Login state, session management
- **Security**: CSRF protection, security monitoring
- **Functionality**: User preferences, platform settings

### Analytics Cookies
- **Usage Analytics**: Platform usage, feature adoption, performance
- **Optimization**: A/B testing, feature improvement, user experience
- **Third-Party Analytics**: Google Analytics, privacy-compliant tracking

### Cookie Management
- **Browser Settings**: Control cookie acceptance and deletion
- **Opt-Out Tools**: Third-party analytics opt-out options
- **Consent Management**: Clear consent mechanisms for non-essential cookies

## Data Retention

### Retention Periods
- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Anonymized after 3 years of inactivity
- **Project Data**: Retained for legal and quality assurance purposes
- **Communication**: Deleted after 7 years unless legally required

### Deletion Process
- **Account Deletion**: Complete removal within 30 days of request
- **Legal Holds**: Some data may be retained for legal compliance
- **Anonymization**: Personal identifiers removed from analytics data
- **Secure Deletion**: Cryptographic erasure of sensitive information

## International Transfers

### Data Processing Locations
- **Primary**: United States (with appropriate safeguards)
- **Backup**: EU-based infrastructure for European users
- **Third Parties**: Vetted providers with adequate protection

### Transfer Safeguards
- **Standard Contractual Clauses**: EU-approved transfer mechanisms
- **Adequacy Decisions**: Transfers to countries with adequate protection
- **Binding Corporate Rules**: Internal data protection standards
- **Consent**: Explicit consent for transfers where required

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

- **Continued Use**: Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
